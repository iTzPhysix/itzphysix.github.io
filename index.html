Development roadmap

Building the foundation before opening the gates.

The current priority is a stable, accurate world client. Multiplayer systems, player identity, battles, and live services follow on top of that foundation.

Roadmap labels describe development focus and do not promise release dates.

NOWWorld foundation

Region rendering, movement, maps, assets, runtime stability, and core client performance.

NEXTPlayer systems

Identity, customization, persistence, party tools, trading, and social presence.

AFTERBattles and progression

Team management, encounters, competitive play, rewards, and long-term progression loops.

ONGOINGLive world expansion

New regions, events, polish, balance, and community-requested improvements.
